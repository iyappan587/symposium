ESSAY WRITING

Rules and Regulations:

  1. Topic will be announced on the spot.
  2. Essay should be in 500 – 600 words.
  3. Time limit is 50 minutes.
  4. Assessment Metrics – Language, Reasoning with evidence, Coherence & Legible handwriting.

MATH QUIZ

Rules and Regulations:

  1. Three people are allowed per team.
  2. Smartphone and calculator usage are prohibited.
  3. Marks will be provided transparently and fairly.
  4. Teams may hold a limited amount of time to respond; after this time, the opportunity will be transferred to another team.
  5. Judges decisions are final

PROJECT-EXPO

Rules and Regulations:

  1. Teams may consist of maximum 2 members, both are from same or inter departments.
  2. Projects should align with the theme and must be original and created by the participants.
  3. Each team will have 10 minutes to present. During the presentation, team members must explain the working principle, all the techniques aspects, parts/components used and highlight the uniqueness of the project.
  4. If the product is patented or applied for patent proof, it must be shown to the judges, which will earn an additional weightage for the project.
  5. The juries evaluate project based on the originality, creativity, technical merit, implementation, clarity of presentation and relevance to the theme.
    6. The decision of the judges will be final, no further disputes will be entertained after the results are declared.
