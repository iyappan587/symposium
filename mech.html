MR.MACHINIST

  1. This event only allows only one participant per entry. If the total number of participants exceeds 25, the competition will be conducted in two rounds.
  2. The first round will be an MCQ test covering the fundamentals of manufactoring processes. The time for MCQ test is 15 mins
  3. The second round will be the actual event with 15 participants. The part drawing with dimensions and raw material are given to the participants. The maximum time limit is 1 hour.
  4. The participants have to make the necessary machining processes and finish the job as per the given dimensions.
  5. The juries evaluate the job based on the time consumptions, surface finish and dimensional accuracy.
  6. The decision of the judges will be the final, and no furthur dusputes will be entertained after the results are declared.

CAD MODELLING

  1. This event allows only one participant per entry. The time limit will be 50 min.
  2. If the participants exceeds morethan 20, then the event will be conducted in two rounds.
  3. The first round will be the online quiz related to planning of building in CAD.The second round will be CAD modeling.
  4. The structure to be drafted will be revealed at the start of the competition.
  5. Create a accurate model represents the design with precise dimensions using layer in CAD software.
  6. After completion of entire building plan,the juries evaluate the design and finalize the participations.
  7. Evaluation criteria for CAD modeling is Accuracy, precision, dimensions, aesthetics, rendering quality, and overall design effectiveness.

Project-Expo

    Rules and Regulations:

  1. Teams may consist of maximum 2 members, both are from same or inter departments.
  2. Projects should align with the theme and must be original and created by the participants.
